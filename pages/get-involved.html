Get Involved

Become Part of the Solution

It all starts with your choice to act instead of looking away. By acknowledging and then addressing the pervasive problem of food insecurity, you start a chain reaction. Struggling families gain the support that they need, granting them a chance at a more promising future, where they in turn can help those still in need. Your action has the power to transform a life, a family, a community, and a nation.

Learn

The key to solving a problem? Understanding it. Learn the facts about food insecurity and the issues impacting the hungry so you can can be an effective Voice for the Hungry.

Engage

Our 867 volunteer voices can help amplify the Voice of the Hungry through Social Media, in our local communities, and by interacting with legislators and decision makers.

Donate

Whether you have five minutes, a few hours, or a bit longer, we need you to stand up and chip in for those in need. Together, we are Hope for Hungry Americans in need.
